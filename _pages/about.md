# Research Interest

I am generally interested in all aspects related to building up functional robotics systems. In particular, I took the most passion in answering the following questions:

* **3D Robot Perception**: Perception enables robot applications to perceive the surroundings and make interactions. Therefore, I wish to develop perception modules with **strong 3D awareness** and **ability to combine multiple sensors**.

* **End-to-end (Perception, Planning, Control) Learning**: Similar to CNNs free people from feature engineering, end-to-end learning the pipeline of perception, planning, and control poses an attractive vision; thus, it is important in academics. However, engineers in the industry prefer building modules independently, as this makes debugging and analysis more clear. Therefore, I am passionate about **end-to-end representation for robots** while **decoding information for debugging and interpretation**.

* **Learning from Human Experiences**: Imitating human behaviors from videos or demonstrations are critical for complex robotics tasks. Therefore, I seek to **find proper interfaces and policies to transfer human knowledge onto robots**.
