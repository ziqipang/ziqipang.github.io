title: "Model-free Vehicle Tracking and State Estimation in Point Cloud Sequences"
collection: publications
permalink: /publication/waymo_sot.md
---
* Studied “Model-free Single-Object Tracking (SOT) in Point Clouds”, targeting on the disadvantages of popular detection-based Multi-Object Tracking approaches in accuracy and robustness.
* Constructed specialized SOT benchmark from Waymo Open Dataset and implemented an effective optimization-based tracking system, which could produce reliable and accurate tracking results even on sparse point cloud frames.
* Completed the 3D shapes of objects and created shape banks by combining point clouds from multiple frames.
* Benefited various applications: LiDAR scan simulation, motion pseudo-ground-truth generation, and optical flow annotation.
