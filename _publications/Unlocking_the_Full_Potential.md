---
title: "Unlocking the Full Potential of Small Data with Diverse Supervision"
collection: publications
permalink: /publication/Unlocking_the_Full_Potential
---

* Studied the problem of representation learning with insufficient training data and proposed to solved this by adding supervision.
* Created a benchmark from ADE20K with specially imitating realistic data distribution and collecting rich types of annotations.
* Proposed a general framework and code-base for combining supervision with multi-task learning and curriculum learning.
* Enhanced the models trained with less data, which could achieve equivalent or better performance using only 50% training data.
